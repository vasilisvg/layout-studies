A fullscreen centered image that doesn't deform.

This image is always fullscreen. When the viewport has a bigger aspect-ratio than the image, part of the image will be cut off from the top and the bottom. If the viewport has a smaller aspect-ratio than the image, part of the image will be cut off from the left and the right side. So, it will always show an incomplete image unless the aspect-ratio of the viewport is exactly the same as the aspect-ratio of the image. Inspired by how the Rijksmuseum shows its collection. (Doesn't work on browsers that don't support the vw and vh unit or the aspect-ratio mediaquery)
