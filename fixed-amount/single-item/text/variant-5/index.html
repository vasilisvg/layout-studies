Single, flexible column with changing percentages, with a rather large max width

This is a simple looking one column layout. The margins to the left and right change a few times, at different viewport sizes. The width of the measure grows from 91% to 60% with a max-width of 44.5em. But there's more.

An interesting thing about this layout is that the first paragraph is 7% wider that the body copy, and the heading is even wider: 15%. This creates a nice, flexible shape on the right side of the text. This layout is copied from this project by IA. There are endless variations possible with these kinds of layouts. Absolutely worth experimenting with.

Looks wonderful while resizing.
