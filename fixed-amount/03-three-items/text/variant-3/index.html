Three items of some importance

Sometimes we want more than one item on a page. For instance, on a blog we might want the articles prominently in the middle, we might want the reactions to the left (if it's a nice community), and we might want to show some widgets to the right. This works on a large screen. On a smaller screen we can show the comments section and the widgets below the main content. We probably have to make them accessible in another way: If we depend on scrolling only most people on a small screen will probably miss it. But in this example we won't do that.
