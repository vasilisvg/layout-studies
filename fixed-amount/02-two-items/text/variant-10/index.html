1. The second item is more important

Sometimes you have an ordered list of two items (or more) where the second item deserves more emphasis, for whatever reason (often marketing, but other reasons can exist). There are several ways to lay this out.

2. Bigger text for the second item

By simply making the font-size of the second item bigger it gets more attention. Very simple, pretty effective. It might be less effective on smaller screens when the second item is not immediately visible.
